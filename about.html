Knowledge Before College Establish in 2020

While a lot of middle school students want to go to high school, most feel scared to do so. A majority of Students tend to feel less prepared for high school.

So one day we came up with this website to inform students in high school and middle school who are interested in going to high school the importace of: grades, gpa, and ect.

We thought it would be a good idea so high schoolers know what to expect for 9-12 grade. We wanted to speak about high school because every grade you get could impact the college you want to go to. dreams.

High school is important because most people who don’t graduate from high school may not be able to achieve their dreams and get money. Yes i know the saying, money can’t buy happiness, but it can buy a house, a car, vacations, food, pay the power bill, the gas bill, and all those things that make life livable.
